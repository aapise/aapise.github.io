About

My name is Ankita Pise and I am a 26-year-old software developer living in San Jose, California. Working as a Software Engineer for IBM Watson's Health Imaging group since 2016, I am passionate about building software that can have a positive impact on the lives of the people around me. In short, I am constantly striving at spreading happiness around using the power of technology, one piece of well-designed software at a time!

I got my Masters in Computer Science from North Carolina State University. Prior to working for IBM, I worked as a Front-end Engineer at Pluma, and helped develop a mobile application for their executive coaching platform.

Creating seamless, intuitive and dynamic user experiences has always been the focus of my work and it is something I strive to achieve in every project I take up.
